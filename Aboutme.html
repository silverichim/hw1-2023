About me

Hey, I am Kai Liu. Welcome! I am a city observer and I like to record life in my eyes with my camera.

Currently, I am a second-year urban graduate student at UT-Austin, focusing on smart cities and landscape cities. I am committed to improving the role of public participation in urban planning, and I like to combine data for quantitative analysis to assist the design process.
